# Gas Estimates

Estimated gas costs for RevvFi operations on Base, Arbitrum, and Ethereum mainnet.

## Overview

Gas costs vary significantly by network:

| Network | Gas Price (typical) | ETH Price | Est. Cost for 100k gas |
|---------|-------------------|-----------|----------------------|
| Base | `0.1–1 Gwei` | $3,500 | `$0.35–3.50` |
| Arbitrum | `0.1–0.5 Gwei` | $3,500 | `$0.35–1.75` |
| Ethereum | `20–100 Gwei` | $3,500 | `$7.00–35.00` |

## Creator Operations

| Component | Gas | Notes |
|-----------|-----|-------|

| Approvals & transfers | 40,000 | Transfer allocations to vaults |

**Costs at Different Networks:**

| Network | Gas Price | Total Cost | Notes |
|---------|-----------|-----------|-------|
| Base | 1 Gwei | $2.00 | Recommended primary network |
| Arbitrum | 0.5 Gwei | $1.00 | Low cost option |

## Gas Optimization Tips

### For Creators

1. **Deploy on Base or Arbitrum first** (~50-80% cheaper than Ethereum)
2. **Deploy during lower network congestion** (reduces base gas price)
3. **Consider batching launches** (if deploying multiple tokens)

### For LPs

1. **Use ETH with lower gas price** (trade off speed vs cost)
2. **Batch governance votes** (combine with other activities)
3. **Withdraw in batches** (if possible with multiple launches)

### For Platforms

1. **Bundle deposits** (if aggregating LP funds)
2. **Execute launches during quiet periods**
3. **Batch governance executions**
